About Us

Intellify strives to create a platform for students to showcase their talent, assess their skills and receive guidance on various ways to enhance their skills. Since its establishment, Intellify has reached out of more than 100 cities, impacting the lives of thousands of teachers and students. So far, it has worked with Delhi Government, NITI Aayog, MHRD (DIC), CBSE, Teach for India, CLI-IIT Gandhinagar and Design Department of IIT

Key Features

We improve engagement with classrooms using following features

Admin & Teacher Dashboard

Enables management of ongoing lectures, tracks and manages the learning progress of the classroom

Student Dashboard

Analyses students’ learning patterns, and provides daily insights for learning

Quiz Generator

Enables continuous assessment through automated quizzes

Learning Analytics

Uses bloom’s taxonomy to assess the progress of learning outcomes

Personalized Homework

Provides customized homework for students based on their current level of understanding

School Level ERP

Allows attendance management, scheduling of lectures, notification management, etc
